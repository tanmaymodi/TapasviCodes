Why Choose CarryLab?

If often happends when someone goes for treatment in a Hospital and they admit that patient but the patient requires high equipped techologies for further treatement. Now that paitent will get dischage from his/her cureent Hospital and then referred to some other well equipped hospital. But patient again has to go with same admission procedures, in such cases CarryLab helps patients and Hospitals by saving by saving their time, money and extra work.

Register Yourself

Referring Patients

Seamless Shifting to another Hospital with minimal efforts

How it works?

Register Yourself

First patient has to register to get admittied in a Hospital

Referral

Suppose there is a need to shift to patient in another Hospital then incharge Staff/Doctor will refer the paitent to appropriate Hospital by filling a form which contains report of your current diagnostics

Acceptance

Patent's new Hospital will accept the request folling proper protocols and also consider the necessary reports from the previous Hospitals Tests and treatment
